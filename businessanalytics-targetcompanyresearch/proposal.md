# Proposal: Building 1000 ICP-Qualified Companies in One Month

## Submitted by
**Name:** Ankur Kumar

**Role Applied:** Business Analytics Internship

## Goal

The objective is to build a verified list of 1000 companies that match the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) within 30 calendar days.

Each company should satisfy the predefined ICP criteria, including industry, company size, growth stage, and relevant decision-makers.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

The target companies should meet the following criteria:

- AI or SaaS companies
- 50–500 employees
- Active product development
- Growing engineering team
- Decision makers available (Founder, CTO, VP Engineering)
- Active website and LinkedIn page

## Data Sources

The following sources will be used:

- LinkedIn
- Crunchbase
- Wellfound
- Product Hunt
- Apollo.io
- GitHub
- Company Websites
- Google Search

## Workflow

The workflow for building the list of 1000 ICP-qualified companies consists of the following steps:

1. Collect company data from multiple trusted sources.
2. Apply predefined ICP filters such as industry, employee size, and growth stage.
3. Remove duplicate companies.
4. Verify company information manually and using AI-assisted tools.
5. Validate decision-maker information.
6. Perform a final quality check.
7. Deliver the final verified list of 1000 companies.

## AI Tools

The following AI tools will be used to improve efficiency and accuracy:

- ChatGPT – Summarize company information and verify ICP matches.
- Claude – Analyze company descriptions and extract useful insights.
- Google Gemini – Research company information and growth signals.
- GitHub Copilot – Assist in writing automation scripts.
- Python – Clean data, remove duplicates, and organize datasets.

## Quality Checks

Each company must pass the following verification checks:

- Active company website
- Valid LinkedIn page
- Correct employee count
- Industry matches the ICP
- Decision-maker information available
- No duplicate entries
- Verified growth signals

## Timeline

| Week | Tasks |
|------|-------|
| Week 1 | Identify data sources and collect 3000–4000 companies |
| Week 2 | Apply ICP filters and remove duplicate companies |
| Week 3 | Verify company information and validate decision-makers |
| Week 4 | Final quality check and prepare the verified list of 1000 companies |

## Deliverables

The final submission will include:

- A verified list of 1000 ICP-qualified companies.
- Company name, website, LinkedIn profile, employee count, industry, and decision-maker details.
- Growth signals and ICP qualification status.
- Documentation of the sourcing and verification process.

## Conclusion

This approach combines trusted business databases, AI-assisted research, and manual verification to efficiently identify and validate 1000 ICP-qualified companies within one month. The workflow emphasizes data accuracy, scalability, and quality, ensuring that the final list is reliable and valuable for business development.
